000 - increment, flags: none
001 - decrement, flags: none
010 - add, flags: carry (C), auxiliary carry (AC), overflow (OV)
011 - add with carry, flags: carry (C), auxiliary carry (AC), overflow (OV)
100 - subb, flags: carry (C), auxiliary carry (AC), overflow (OV)
101 - multiply, flags: carry (C), overflow (OV)
110 - divide, flags: carry (C),  overflow (OV)
111 - no operation, flags: none
